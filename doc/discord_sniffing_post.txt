# Want to Help? Become a Sniffer!

The WoW emulation community needs **your help** capturing data from retail servers to make private servers as accurate and complete as possible.

**What is sniffing?** You run a small background tool while you play WoW retail normally on your own Battle.net account. It silently records all the game data (NPC positions, quest text, vendor inventories, etc.) into a file. You share that file, and it gets turned into database entries. That's it!

**Is it safe?** Yes — the tool (Ymir) only reads network traffic at the operating system level. It never touches WoW's files, memory, or process. Your game doesn't even know it's there. Zero bans have been documented in 7+ years of community use.

**What you need:**
- A WoW retail subscription (you're just playing the game normally)
- ~5 minutes to set up two free tools (one-time)
- The willingness to click on NPCs and explore zones

**Midnight content we especially need:**
- Silvermoon City (the rebuilt hub — walk through everything!)
- Eversong Woods, Zul'Aman, Harandar, Voidstorm
- Campaign quests and dungeons
- Every vendor, trainer, and flight master you can find
